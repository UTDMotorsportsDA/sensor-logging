Instructions:

To contribute, please do the following:

* Read the module you want to claim
* Fill in your name and expected completion date in the spreadsheet
* Develop and test
* Organize source and documentation
* Commit your code to GitHub (always pull changes before you push!)
* Mark your module as completed in the spreadsheet.

Take advantage of anything and everything in the car software you think might help.

Java's full reference documentation is available at https://docs.oracle.com/javase/8/ (or you can Google "class_name se 8").
